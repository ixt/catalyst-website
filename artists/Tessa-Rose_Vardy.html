Tessa-Rose Vardy

Tessa-Rose Vardy a South African born artist, is interested in shaped spaces, data and their relationship to one another in the physical world. She brings light to these topics by using data visulization. void drawShape () is an installation dealing with the three themes mentioned above. Tessa is bringing data into the gallery space in forms of three shapes and trying to bring a personal reflection to the viewers reaction. By using data taken from the internet she has an interest in data accessibility and how she can transform that into something tangible.
