Jordan Wu

Jordan is a British-born artist whose past work in photorealistic pieces have developed into themes of recreation of reality. This is reflected through his past pieces such as Recursive Flaws as well as his recent piece Door To Door, an immersive three-dimensional environment, experienced through virtual reality, with the aim of fully immersing the audience in a dystopian yet familiar world.
Negative Flux is a virtual reality piece that revolves around themes of negative spacing and explores flows between the physical and virtual, specialising on focus and presence in space.
