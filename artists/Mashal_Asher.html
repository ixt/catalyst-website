Mashal Asher

Mashal Asher is a international artist, who is interested in the human gaze and the intricacies of communication in different languages. Her ideas explore human behaviour and the concept of surveillance. ‘‘#001O.D’’ is an installation depicting the Syrian boy, Omar Daqneesh and his tragic appearance on the media during the war in Syria. The piece depicts Omar Daqneesh out of scrap materials combined to create an interactive piece that gives the viewer a robotic depiction of war.
