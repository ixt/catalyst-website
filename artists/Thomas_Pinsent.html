Thomas Pinsent

Born, raised and still studying in London, Tom has a deep love for all things musical. He enjoys exploring methods to combine this with his interests in visual art and technology, producing ideas and work sat somewhere between all three. Tom’s piece is an immersive audio environment that makes use of visitors’ movements throughout the gallery, generating music that reflects the fluctuations and rhythms of the space in real-time.
