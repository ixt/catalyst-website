Fathima Thasleem

Nazreen is an East London born digital artist that has a strong interest in aesthetic visuals. She is mainly inspired by nature and its surroundings.
Her work involves growth and interaction. She will be displaying a projection piece with microphone generated graphics. It is a form of interactive digital art, attracting those who are intrigued by the amalgamation of audio and graphics. As the tree sprouts you see the beauty of nature and as it falls silent you feel the isolation.
