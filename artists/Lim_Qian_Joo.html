Lim Qian Joo

Qian Joo Lim is a Singapore-born artist. Lim’s works generally do not have a central theme but her interests lie in experimenting with interactive interfaces and paradoxical concepts through different computational tools and media.
Lim’s artwork is both an apparatus and user interface. It tries to raise questions about the belief of the veracity of information presented in our everyday digital lives through disrupting the digital interface.
