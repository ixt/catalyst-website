Alma Ulmane

Alma was born in Latvia but has spent most of her life living in Belgium and more recently London. Her work is strongly influenced by the mixture of cultures, beliefs and ideologies she has been exposed to from a young age.
Her current work is her exploration into her Latvian culture and heritage. It juxtaposes the traditional values of dainas (traditional Latvian poetry), signs and traditional production methods with computational language analysis and various drawing algorithms.
