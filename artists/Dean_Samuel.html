Dean Samuel

Dean Samuel is a hackney born digital artist with a strong interest in movies, games and animations and how they can be considered a form of art. He generally focuses less on the concept and dematerialisation of art, instead he tries to create work he genuinely finds visually pleasing.
The majority of his work questions whether video games can be seen as a form of art, his projects have shifted from being drawing/painting based to programming based with some level of interactivity over the recent years. This year he will be looking into how a game like environment can be displayed in different ways to represent different genres.
