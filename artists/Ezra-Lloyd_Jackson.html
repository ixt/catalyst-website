Ezra-Lloyd Jackson was raised in South-East London. @_333xo is a two part exhibition with a live stream in Goldsmiths of an external event.(Where is the event taking place? How do we make sense of distributed knowledge and simultaneity? How does a piecemeal assemblage of likes and swipes start to make me you us?). The piece explores kinship, identification, vilification, aesthetic turn ons and turn offs, scent perfume jewels and lures, deceptions, glamour, disgust. Using machine labour, code, plastic, metal and Instagram.
