Djamel Ouddane

Djamel Ouddane is a London based artist with a background in tattooing and illustration. He has spent the majority of his life exploring various mediums be it paper, skin or computing and loves honest and thought provoking concepts that allow others to be inspired.
Heavily influenced by games, manga and mythology of the world the various opportunities that technology provides has become an outlet for his developing ideas. “Arcane” is an installation that uses the coding language of C++ to produce a 3D projected hologram of moving particles within a transparent prism.
