Haruna Sawai

Born in Japan and raised in London, Haruna Sawai studies Digital Art at Goldsmiths, University of London. Haruna is experimenting to redefine the legitimacy of Digital Art using 3D visuals. This year her work revolves around creating graphic based projection mapping that can capture human movements by working with motion sensors.
