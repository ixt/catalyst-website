Emily Crosby

Emily Crosby is an artist from Cambridge who is interested in nature, digital graphics, patterns, and illustration.
Her work looks at the way plants grow and uses real life data, collected from the plant, to model and generate new plants. The plants are then scored using her own personal judgement and given a message based on the score given. The new plant models are generated using a genetic algorithm and then depicted as hand drawn illustrations and digital drawings.
