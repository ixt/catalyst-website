Maria Loks-Thompson

Maria Loks-Thompson is a Polish born digital artist. Her work is strongly informed by the relationship between the political and private spheres of everyday life. Security Footage is a mixed-media installation consisting of found objects, and live and pre-recorded video footage. It provides the audience with an intimate experience based around the feeling of anxiety and its increasing presence within contemporary society.
